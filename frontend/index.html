Our Story

Baked Fresh, Made
with Purpose

Goshen Home Bakery was born from a simple belief: that every celebration deserves a cake that's as special as the moment itself. Started from our family kitchen in Maseno, we've grown into a beloved local institution without losing the handcrafted soul.

Every tier, every frosting swirl, every sugar flower is shaped by hand because shortcuts have no place when love is the main ingredient.
